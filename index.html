Mohammad Soltanshah

Researcher | Software Developer | Instructor

I am an instructor and PhD candidate in Engineering Science at Simon Fraser University, specializing in machine learning for robotic grasping and visual servoing in robotic manipulation. My Ph.D. thesis focuses on deep reinforcement learning application for precise robotic grasping and image-space path planning for redundant visual servoing in aerial manipulation. Previously, I completed my M.Sc. at Isfahan University of Technology, where my thesis focused on SLAM-based navigation for ground mobile robots in dynamic/unstructured environements.
