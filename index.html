About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Engineering Science at Simon Fraser University, specializing in machine learning for robotic grasping and visual servoing in robotic manipulation. Previously, I completed my M.Sc. at Isfahan University of Technology, where my thesis focused on SLAM-based navigation for ground mobile robots.
